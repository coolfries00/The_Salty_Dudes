Anthony Hernandez

Anthony started thinking of his future career when he was just 13. He was thinking of a job, and that's when The Salty Dudes idea started. He had to move on with school before anything happened, but he still thought about it. throughout his years he thought of it as a simple restaurant, but he wanted to make it special. After he graduated from high school he had it all planned out, a multicultural restaurant, which would be a restaurant that served food that were from 2 different cultures, but put them together. He then went to college and graduated with a Masters degree in cooking. As an adult now he started to build The Salty Dudes. He built it all on his own for 3 years. All that handwork payed off when his restaurant began to go viral and it became the first multicultural restaurant in the whole world.
